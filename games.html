Slug Jumper

Embark on the Journey of a Lifetime

You are the Slug Boy, a slug who has aspirations for great adventure. You will embark on a journey across the bug kingdom in order to achieve your potential. Tired of being told you're nothing but a lowly slug, you will travel across the great backyard to the tree. A mystical beacon that all bugs and backyard life aspire to reach. At the top holds untold power, something that you as the Slug Boy wish to reach.

Run and Jump Across many Obstacles

The journey won't be easy, for it is a treacherous path to reach the tree. You will have to endure many hardships, avoid salt, jump over spikes, and outmaneuver your enemies. You are no fighter, but that doesn't mean you don't have a few tricks up your sleeve! As the Slug Boy, you have an inherent ability to jump, climb, and run great distances to avoid your foes. But be careful, for there are stories of a great beast lurking around the base of the tree, ready to take on any foolish enough to aproach it...

Enter the Crypt

Protect your Kingdom from Dark Forces

Evil forces lurk in the world, and they're coming for your kingdom! The evil army of the underworld is coming for your people, and it is up to you to stop them! In this thrilling game, you must do everything in your power to ensure your kingdom lives another day. You will embark in a thrilling tower defense game where it is up to you to shore up your defenses and protect all that you hold dear. Only you can do this! Apparently the other strategists tookt he day off...

Use Strategy to Outwit your Foes

At your disposal is the best technology your kingdom has to offer. Place towers across the map to stop the invading forces from reaching your castle. Place them in spots where they won't be able to proceed. Choose from a variety of different tower types to mow down the enemy. From a tower that locks on to the enemy to one that slows them down, there are a number of towers for you to choose from! But be warned, there have been tales that the army of the underworld have their own tricks up their sleevees...
